Major Works of Satyajit Ray

1.Feluda Series (Detective Fiction)

Feludar Goendagiri (1965) – First Feluda story introducing the detective and his cousin Topshe.

Sonar Kella (1971) – Feluda investigates a boy’s claim of remembering a past life.

Joy Baba Felunath (1975) – Mystery set in Varanasi involving a stolen idol.

Other titles: Badshahi Angti, Kailashey Kelenkari, Joto Kando Kathmandu-te, Darjeeling Jomjomat.

Collected Editions: The Complete Adventures of Feluda (Vol. 1 & 2).

2.Professor Shonku Series (Science Fiction)

Professor Shonku o Aschorjo Putul – Adventures with strange inventions.

Ek Shringo Obhijan – Journey to find a unicorn.

Shonku o Adbhut Rajar Deshe – Encounters with a mysterious king.

Collected Editions: The Diary of Professor Shonku.

3.Children’s Novels

Fatik Chand (1978) – A boy loses his memory and joins a circus troupe.

Bankubabur Bandhu (1962) – A short science fiction story about an alien befriending a schoolteacher

Pagla Dashu – Humorous tales about a quirky boy.

4.Short Story Collections

Indigo (Collection) – Stories of horror, fantasy, and satire.

Baro Bhuter Gappo – Ghost stories.

Galpa 101 – Anthology of 101 stories.

5.Tarini Khuro Stories

Narratives of Tarini Khuro (Uncle Tarini), who tells bizarre and supernatural adventure stories.
